How To Read This

Elapsed bucket tells you when the bet fired on the full NHL game clock. This recap now groups bets into 1-minute windows across all 65 minutes of regulation plus overtime. For example, 20:00-21:00 means the signal fired during that one-minute stretch. The underlying ledger also keeps 15-second bins for deeper tuning.

Driver tags are the machine-readable reasons the signal liked a bet at that moment. Examples: pace:up means the game is running faster than expected, goalie:weak means the save environment looks softer than baseline, and manpower:pp_home means the home team is on a power play.

Tag types are the families before the colon. So pace groups pace-related tags, goalie groups goalie-strength tags, and gate groups rule or threshold tags that let a bet through.
